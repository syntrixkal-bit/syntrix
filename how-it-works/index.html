Tell us about your home or business and what you want assurance over. We recommend the right assessment, agree a fixed price, and schedule a time that suits you.

No obligation, no jargon — just a short conversation about your setup and concerns.

We assess your network

With your signed authorisation in place, we examine your network the way an attacker would — encryption, router configuration, connected devices, coverage and exposure.

Step-by-step remediation — with an optional retest to confirm it worked.

The ground rules

Built-in safeguards, on every engagement.

These aren't optional extras — they're conditions we place on ourselves before we accept any work.

Written authorisation first

Before anything is assessed, we agree a precise scope in writing and both sign an authorisation agreement. It protects you legally under the Computer Misuse Act 1990 — and it means nothing ever happens without your explicit consent.

You stay in control

You choose the scope, the timing and the boundaries. If we find something critical mid-assessment, we pause and tell you immediately rather than pressing on.

Everything is explained

Before, during and after — you'll always know what we're doing and why. We're happy to walk through the report with you line by line once it's delivered.

Evidence, not opinions

Every finding in your report is verified and documented with evidence. If something is in good shape, the report says that too.

Aftercare included

Questions after delivery are part of the service, not an extra. And when you've made the fixes, a retest can confirm the gaps are genuinely closed.

In writing

Our full authorisation terms are public. Read the Authorisation Agreement before you ever sign it.

Typical timeline: home audits are completed in a single visit with the report delivered shortly after — often within 48 hours. Business audits are scoped to your site before we begin.

Ready when you are

Know what’s connected. Know what’s exposed. Know what to fix.

One audit. One clear report. Complete clarity about the network everything in your life runs through.
