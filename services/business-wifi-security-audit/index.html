Business WiFi Security Audits

Wireless security your stakeholders can rely on.

For a business, an insecure wireless network is a direct route past your other defences. Guest traffic mingling with corporate systems, forgotten access points, weak segmentation and default credentials are exactly what an attacker looks for. We assess your wireless estate the way an adversary would, then translate the findings into documentation your leadership and technical teams can both act on.
